Rules & Regulations

All members must present their MemberID to gain entry and check-in to the Sweat Zone.
For non-members, kindly purchase a one-day gym pass at the counter or sign up for free

You must wear appropriate sports attire when using the Sweat Zone. Jeans, boots, heels, flip-flops, or revealing clothing are not permitted.
It is advisable to wear sportswear made of comfortable fabrics, while restrictive garments like denim are discouraged.
Additionally, using gym equipment barefoot is not allowed.

Upon Entry, you may collect the key for the locker from the marshals in charge.
For members:
no charge will be imposed
For non-members:
a rental fee of RM0.50 will be imposed
However, please note that the lockers are limited and operate on a first-come-first-serve basis.

You are responsible for your own safety while in the Sweatzone.
It is highly recommended that you warm up and cool down before and after your workout.
Please navigate to our workout videos page for guidance.
