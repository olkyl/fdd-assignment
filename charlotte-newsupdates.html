Hey everyone! I'm Rama. Juggling lectures, labs, and late-night study sessions can be demanding, but I've discovered that healthy eating has been instrumental in sustaining my energy and focus.

Tips for Healthy Eating on Campus:

  1. Start Your Day on a High Note:

    2. Energize your mornings with a nutritious breakfast to kickstart your day. Opt for options like oatmeal with fresh fruit, whole-grain toast with peanut butter, or yogurt parfaits from the campus cafeteria.

  2. Snack Smartly Throughout the Day:

    3. Combat the mid-day slump by keeping nutritious snacks at hand. Pack portable options like trail mix, protein bars, or sliced veggies with hummus to stave off hunger between classes.

  3. Stay Hydrated Throughout the Day:

    4. Hydration is essential for staying focused and alert. Keep a refillable water bottle with you and sip on water regularly throughout the day, especially during busy study sessions.
